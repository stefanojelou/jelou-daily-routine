### 🆕 Signups flatlined ~90% since Jul 3 — anomaly isolated to signup_completed

The biggest WoW mover across everything Mixpanel can see: production signup_completed collapsed from a stable ~61/day June baseline to just 3, 4, 3, 10 per day on Jul 4-7 (~92% below trend; last 7d 188 vs prior 7d 571 = -67% WoW). Ruled out two benign explanations — it is NOT an environment-tag drift (the all-environment $overall count collapsed identically) and signups did NOT move to the marketing website project (3842156 records zero signup_completed). That leaves two live hypotheses: a broken signup_completed instrumentation/event (tracking regression) or a genuine new-signup funnel outage. Either way it needs eyes today, and while unresolved every signup-based cohort metric in this project is unreliable.
